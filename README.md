# Rigour Hero -- Player Guide

## What is Rigour Hero?

Rigour Hero is a browser-based prayer flicking arcade game inspired by Old School RuneScape. You pick a boss, switch your prayers to block incoming attacks, build combos, earn XP, collect pets, and climb the leaderboard. It runs entirely in your browser at [rigourhero.com](https://rigourhero.com) -- no download, no account creation, just type your RSN and go.

---

## How to Play

1. Go to [rigourhero.com](https://rigourhero.com).
2. Type your RuneScape name and press Enter.
3. Pick a boss from the boss selector. Pick a game mode from the mode selector (top right).
4. Press Space to start the run.
5. Prayer orbs fly toward you. Click the correct prayer to block each attack before it hits. Every correct block earns XP and extends your combo. Every miss costs HP. When your HP runs out, the run ends and your score is submitted.

Your score equals your total XP earned that run. XP per block starts at 10 and increases by 2 for every combo you build (10, 12, 14, 16...). Longer combos mean significantly higher scores.

## Game Modes

| Mode | HP | Lanes | Speed | Description |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Classic** | 3 | 1 | Normal | The standard experience. Three lives, one lane, full prayer HUD. Good for learning the game and grinding XP. |
| **Challenge** | 1 | 1 | Normal | Same lane, same speed -- but one mistake kills you. Increased pet chance. |
| **Impossible (IM)** | 1 | 3 | Fast | Three lanes of orbs, faster travel time, one life. The first few orbs come in slower as a warmup, then the real speed kicks in. |
| **Hardcore Impossible (HCIM)** | 1 | 5 | Fastest | Five lanes. One tick travel time. One life. No mercy. Best pet rates in the game. |

All modes have separate leaderboards. You can cycle between them on the leaderboard screen, or view "All Modes" combined.

## GP (Grindy Points... or something)

GP is the in-game currency. Nobody can quite remember what it stands for. Every time GP is mentioned in a drop message, the game picks a random name from a pool of possibilities -- Grindy Points, Gold Points, Guthix Points, Gnome Points, Gilded Platelegs, Goblin Points... it changes every time. The game itself isn't sure.

**How to earn GP:**

- **XP = GP.** Every point of XP you earn in a run is also earned as GP, 1:1. A 50k XP run earns 50k GP.
- **Ground finds.** Every blocked orb has a 1/2000 chance to find 10,000 GP on the ground (20,000 with Ring of Wealth).
- **Boss drops.** Each boss has a unique drop table. Items you receive are automatically converted to GP. The rarer the item, the more it's worth.

## Collection Log

Every boss has a unique drop table based on real OSRS items. Each time you block an orb, you roll against the active boss's drop table. If you hit, the item is added to your collection log and converted to GP.

- Check your clog: open your Profile, click the **Clog** tab.
- Each boss shows how many unique items you've obtained out of the total.
- Click a boss to see all possible drops with their rates and GP values.
- New items trigger a notification in chat: *"New item added to your collection log!"*
- Duplicates still convert to GP, but won't re-add to the log.

Your boss selection matters -- you can only receive drops from the boss you're fighting.

---

## Pets

Every correct prayer block rolls for a pet from the boss you're fighting. Pet rates scale with mode difficulty -- harder modes give better odds. You can only earn one pet per boss per run.

## Account Security

You can set a password on your account so nobody else can log in with your RSN.

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Click **Set Account Password**.
3. An admin will enable password auth on your account.
4. Next time you log in, you'll be prompted to create your password.

Accounts with 1M+ total XP can request password protection at any time.

## Tips

- **Buy Ring of Wealth first.** It doubles your GP ground finds.
- **Boss selection matters.** Each boss has its own drop table -- pick the right boss for the items you want.
- **Alt-drag your HUD.** Move your HP orb, prayer orb, and displays wherever you want.
- **Combo is everything.** XP per block scales with combo (10 + combo x 2). A 100-combo streak earns 210 XP per block.
- **Harder modes = better pet rates.** If you're hunting pets, Challenge and above give better odds.
- **Watch for ground finds.** Gold chat message when you find GP mid-run. Rare but feels great.
